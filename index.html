Monkey

  Chimpanzee

Chimpanzees are mammals also they are the most human like monkey species. They mostly live in west and center of Africa. Chimpanzees mostly live on high trees. Their average life-time is from 40 to 45 years. Chimpanzees live in troops that count about 60 individuals. They mostly eat meat, insects, fruits and plants.

 




 

  Gorilla

Gorillas are also mammals but they are much larger than chimpanzees. Gorilas are herbivorous that's mean that they only eat plants, leafs and fruits. The average life-time of gorilla is around 35 years. Gorillas lead a peaceful lifestyle, most of their time they spent in familiy groups. Gorillas are able to use rocks as tools to open nuts.

 




 

  Gibbon

Gibbons are mammals. Their weight is about 8 kilo grams and height about 1 meter. They live in small groups that count from 5 to 7 individuals. Gibbons are active at day when they are looking for food such as fruits, leafs and plants. Gibbons spent their lifes on trees. Average life-time of gibbon is 25 years.
